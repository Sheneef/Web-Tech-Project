Crunchyroll is streaming My Hero Academia Season 7 during the spring anime season

As the My Hero Academia anime returns with fresh episodes in the spring of 2024, the call for heroes has once again been issued! According to crunchy roll Four special episodes, featuring new scenes that set up the seventh season, will begin with My Hero Academia: Memories on April 6 at 2:30 a.m. PT.

These episodes will look back at the series' most spectacular moments to date. The upcoming episodes of My Hero Academia: Memories are scheduled to premiere on April 13, 20, and 27. On May 4 at 2:30 a.m. PT, brand-new episodes of My Hero Academia Season 7 will subsequently premiere.
