Sign of Affection

About:

From the moment the opening credits grace the screen, "Sign of Affection" envelops viewers in a world that challenges conventional notions of intimacy. At the heart of this narrative beats the story of Yuki Himuro, a young woman who has found solace and understanding in the embrace of silence. Born deaf, Yuki navigates life's complexities with a quiet strength, her expressive eyes and gentle mannerisms conveying more than words ever could.

Fate's serendipitous hand intertwines Yuki's path with that of Itaru Sugimoto, a seemingly aloof individual who harbors a deep appreciation for the nuances of sign language. As their worlds collide, a bond begins to form a connection that transcends the barriers of spoken communication and delves into the realm of unspoken understanding.

As Yuki and Itaru navigate the complexities of their blossoming relationship, they are confronted with societal perceptions, personal insecurities, and the weight of expectations all of which serve to deepen the emotional resonance of their journey.

Embrace the silence, for "Sign of Affection" is about to whisper its way into your soul, revealing the profound depths of love's silent language and leaving an indelible mark on your heart.
