7th Time Loop: The Villainess Enjoys a Carefree Life Married to Her Worst Enemy!

About:

At the heart of this intriguing narrative is Rishe, the duke's daughter, a young woman no stranger to the cycle of reincarnation. Having lived through six previous lives, each one restarting at the pivotal moment of her broken engagement, Rishe finds herself thrust into her seventh incarnation, armed with the knowledge and experiences of her past existences.

Yet, this life promises to be unlike any other, as a fateful encounter with a prince one who had taken her life in a previous iteration sets in motion a chain of events that will challenge Rishe's perceptions and force her to confront the very nature of her reality.

To prevent the outbreak of war and secure a life of leisure, Rishe finds herself walking the precarious path of becoming the bride of an enemy nation's prince, forging an alliance that defies the boundaries of allegiance and tradition. As she navigates the complexities of her new reality, Rishe must confront the ghosts of her past lives, unravel the mysteries that bind her to this intricate tapestry of fate, and ultimately, embrace the power to shape her own destiny.
