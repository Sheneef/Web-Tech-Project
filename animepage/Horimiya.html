Horimiya

About:

From its charming characters to its touching moments, Horimiya invites viewers into the lives of high school students as they navigate the up s and downs of adolescence and relationships. At the heart of the story are Hori Kyouko and Miyamura Izumi, two seemingly mismatched classmates who discover that there is more to each other than meets the eye.

As Hori and Miyamura peel back the layers of their personalities, they uncover vulnerabilities and insecurities that they never dared to share with anyone else. Through their blossoming friendship and eventual romance, they learn to embrace their true selves and find acceptance in each other's arms.

From the outgoing Hori to the reserved Miyamura, from the brash Ishikawa to the bookish Yoshikawa, every character brings something unique to the table, enriching the tapestry of the story and making it feel vibrant and alive.

Through Hori and Miyamura's relationship, the series celebrates the beauty of finding someone who loves you for who you truly are, flaws and all. It's a reminder that true love knows no bounds and that sometimes, the most unexpected connections can lead to the deepest connections.
