Kengan Ashura

About:

Prepare to be thrust into a world where corporations settle their disputes not in boardrooms, but in the brutal arena of underground gladiatorial combat. Created by Yabako Sandrovich and illustrated by Daromeon, Kengan Ashura delivers a knockout punch of intense fights, complex characters, and a gripping narrative that will keep you on the edge of your seat from the first episode to the last.

Kengan Ashura revolves around Tokita Ohma, a young martial artist with exceptional skills, who finds himself thrust into the heart of the Kengan matches. These battles aren't just about brawn; they're a clash of ideologies, strategies, and sheer willpower. Ohma's journey through the world of underground fighting is a visceral rollercoaster ride, as he faces off against a diverse array of opponents, each with their own unique fighting style and motivations.

Ohma is not alone in his quest for victory. Along the way, he forms alliances with a colorful cast of characters, from seasoned veterans to enigmatic newcomers, each with their own reasons for stepping into the ring. As friendships are forged and rivalries ignite, viewers are treated to a spectacle of martial prowess that will leave them breathless.

Kengan Ashura isn't just about flashy fight scenes; it's also a story about the human spirit and the lengths to which people will go to achieve their goals. As the series unfolds, viewers will find themselves drawn deeper into a world of corporate intrigue, personal vendettas, and the quest for power.
