A Girl & her Guard Dog

About:

"A Girl & Her Guard Dog" is a captivating anime that explores the complex world of 15-year-old Senagaki Isaku, the granddaughter of a notorious yakuza boss, as she navigates the delicate balance between her family's notorious legacy and her desire for a normal life.

Isaku's struggle, as she grapples with the weight of her family's reputation, casting a long shadow over her dreams of making friends, attending high school, and perhaps even finding love. Despite her yearning for a ordinary teenage existence, she finds herself inextricably tied to a world of honor, loyalty, and the unwritten rules that govern the yakuza underworld.
